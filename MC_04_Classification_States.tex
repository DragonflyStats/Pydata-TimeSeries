\subsection*{Absorbing States}
\begin{itemize}
\item Absorbing states are characterized in Markov chains by a value of 1 in the diagonal
element of the matrix ($p_{ii} = 1$).
\item Once entered, there is no escaping the absorbing
states.
\end{itemize}
